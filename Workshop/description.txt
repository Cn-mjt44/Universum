Universum is a work-in-progress [b]outer space framework[/b]. Universum's features include [b]automatic caching[/b] for performance and [b]customizability[/b], as every module added through this will be toggleable in the mod settings. A wiki with what features it can provide and how to utilize these in your mod will be provided soon.
